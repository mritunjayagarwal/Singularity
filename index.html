SUPERNOVA

The Beginning of the End

Supernova is a blockchain-based Play-to-earn gaming ecosystem built with Web3 and Unity 3D.

Our ecosystem is roused by an alien invasion on our mother planet Earth, which denotes the beginning of the end. The game aims to consolidate military realism with hard sci-fi and the weirdness of an alien intrusion. This gives the player pinpoint control of the defenses while keeping a feeling of pace and danger.

The arising legends of tomorrow await you. Become a part of our community and save the earth.

PLAY TO EARN

Complete your daily tasks, plot missions and play with others in PVP gameplay. Command your armada, gain experience points and improve your battleships to level up. Top of all, contend with others in ranking competitions to get $SPNV as rewards.
